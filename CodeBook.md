### Codebook

A description of the data variables used in the tiny dataset.

###### subject
	definition: represents the number of the person who was being evaluated  
	type: integer  
	range: 1, 30  
<br />
###### activity
	definition: describes the type of activity that the subject was performing while the data was collected  
	type: factor  
	range:  
		WALKING  
		WALKING_UPSTAIRS  
		WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS  
		SITTING  
		STANDING  
		LAYING  
<br />
###### measurements
